Free Bluesky Tools

A collection of free and open-source Bluesky tools, with every JavaScript created using AI, aka vibe coding. No ads, no paywalls and no trackers. Just simple and useful tools for everyone.

New Horizons

Real-time news from independent sources, journalists, and institutions, powered by Bluesky AppView API. Available as a PWA.

Download Bluesky Video

Easily download Bluesky videos in available resolutions or get the playlist URL to stream. Respect authors' privacy and give proper credits.

Bluesky Trending Topics

Explore Bluesky's trending topics, from past to present. Bluesky is becoming the next big thing. A moderated space where people gather to discuss what's happening.

Bluesky User Statistics

Enter a Bluesky username to see stats on posts, likes, reposts, and follows. View hashtag, mention, link, and word usage, along with their counts.

Bluesky Advanced Search

A simple tool to search Bluesky posts, users, and tags. Just type what you need, and it'll fill the specific formats.

Bluesky Post Analyzer

Enter a Bluesky post URL to analyze posts with detailed stats. Track likes, reposts, replies, and quotes. View engagement timelines, user interactions, and performance metrics.

Download Bluesky Repo and Blobs

Downloading a CAR (Content Addressable aRchives) file may take some time, depending on the account size. Once downloaded, it is converted into JSON format, allowing you to view stats like the number of likes, reposts, etc. You can also download Bluesky blobs (media files) separately.

Export Posts from Bluesky

Choose profile-based exports, including followers, following, and lists, or use any profile, list, or feed link for customized downloads. You can also enter a Bluesky post link to download the full metadata of its thread.

Bluesky Spotlight Feeds

Discover curated Bluesky feeds and lists, including recent or trending news, trending topics, tech updates, space insights, movies, shows, and popular profiles.

PhotoStream

An Instagram-like feed for Bluesky with sorted posts, regular updates, and tailored categories like photography, art, nature, and more.

Bookmark Bluesky Posts

Bookmark Bluesky posts with a Telegram bot and save them in a Google Sheet. A website to display a feed full of Bluesky posts that you curated. Make your personalized feed public by using the script, Telegram bot, and this template.

Everything is deployed on Github Pages, with the JavaScript for each project created using LLMs. Read more at my blog.
