Bluesky Trending Feeds

This is a collection of feeds created by @trending.bsky.app on Bluesky to give users an idea of what's trending on the platform. Feeds may be archived as snapshots after trends fade. Right now, feeds don’t have a createdAt field, only indexedAt, which indicates when the record was first seen. Click the buttons to view posts on Bluesky or news feeds on search engines.

This website showcases Bluesky's trending topics. Currently, the only way to view the trends is by visiting the dedicated trending account on Bluesky and checking the feeds created by that account. My goal was to create a calendar-based display for easier navigation of trending topics by date. However, due to some limitations, like the absence of a createdAt field for feeds, I ended up building it this way. Maybe I'll revisit this project in the future with new ideas. Bluesky is becoming the next big thing, like a new talking square where everyone gathers to talk about what's happening, without any hate speech or disruptive behavior, like a moderated space.
